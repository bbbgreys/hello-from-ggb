WELCOME TO MY HOBBIES!



There are so many things that I like to do in my life.
I love painting, drawing, singing, vlogging, cooking and talking.
I learned how to draw when I was in elementary. I started using color pencil
I also tried watercolor pencil then used watercolor when I was in junior high school.
In senior high school, I learned to make portraits using graphite and charcoal
Right now, I am trying to use different medium like digital, oil, acrylic, etc.

I learned how to cook, when I started living in dorm. I had to learn and I really
enjoyed cooking for me and my friends! I just watch youtube videos or search on google
if I want to cook something. I'm so glad that people like what I cook.

I sang in church and sometimes cover some songs with my cousin. I sometimes upload them
on my youtube channel. I love taking videos and editing. I am planning on having
my own art exhibit in the future!
